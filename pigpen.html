    • Other notes and tiddlybits...

    Have you also tried...
  • ... looking at the other puzzle event materials? (e.g. DASH physical components)
  • ... checking if any hidden words stretch across 2 words, or inside words?
  • ... considering cryptic crossword clues?
  • ... looking for double letters? Missing letters? Shared letters?
  • ... looking for Palindromes? Homophones? Rhymes? Synonyms? Antonyms?
  • ... looking from a distance? (do shaded areas spell letters?)
  • ... thinking if any word parts are thematically related? (DASH 7 tea leaves -- camo + mile = chamomile tea)
  • ... guessing the pattern of the solution answer based on previous puzzle answers? (same letter start, pun on phrase, etc)
  • ... asking if any of the weird words are song or band titles? Company or product names? Sports teams?
  • Binary: 0s and 1s (and letters that resemble them, e.g. I, l, o, O), 2 unique characters, "bits", "bytes", shaded or provided elements vs. empty
  • Braille: 2x3 grids, dots, "sight", "see", "feel", "dark"
  • Morse: sound, audio, hear, audio, dit, dah, dash, tele-gram, 2 unique characters or occurrences of single vs double/triple
  • Ternary: 3 unique characters, or occurences of 0 vs. 1 vs. 2
  • Pigpen: dots and lines (but not Morse or Mayan numerals), pig, pen, porcine
  • Semaphore: 3x3 grids with center dot, clock hands/times, "flag", "signal", raising or lowering hands/arms, circle of 8, angle with orientation/direction indicated
